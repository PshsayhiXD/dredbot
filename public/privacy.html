Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how it's used.

1. What We Collect

  • Discord ID: When you create an account via Discord.

2. Why We Collect It

  • To verify your identity.

3. Discord Verification

When a new IP logs in, we notify your Discord account for manual approval.

4. Data Retention

We retain only data necessary for functionality and security. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Third Parties

We use Discord for login. See their privacy policy.

6. Data Security

We take the protection of your data seriously. All sensitive information is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not share, sell, or leak your data. Access is strictly limited to what is required to provide and secure the Dredbot service.

7. Children's Privacy

Dredbot is intended for users of Discord and games rated 13+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Your Rights (GDPR and CCPA)

  • The right to access the data we hold about you.
  • The right to correct inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.
  • The right to object to certain data processing.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via Discord: pshsayhi4117.

9. Cookies

We use cookies only to support login and session management. These cookies help keep you signed in automatically and maintain your account security. They do not track your browsing activity outside of Dredbot, and we do not use cookies for advertising or analytics purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after updates means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.

11. Contact

Contact us via Discord for any data or privacy-related requests: pshsayhi4117 or our Discord Server.

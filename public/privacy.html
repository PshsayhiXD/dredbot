Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2025

1. Overview

At Dredbot, we respect your privacy and handle your data responsibly. We collect only what's necessary to provide a secure and functional service. This Privacy Policy outlines how your information is used, stored, and protected - as well as how you can manage or delete it anytime.

2. Information We Collect

Discord Account Information

When you log in using Discord, Dredbot receives limited account data from Discord's OAuth2 API:

  • Your Discord User ID
  • Your Discord username and optional avatar
  • Guild (server) IDs where Dredbot operates

Session IDs

Each login or password recovery request generates a random, anonymous sessionId. This sessionId helps us verify your current session securely without collecting personally identifiable information like IP addresses or device details.

Temporary IP Data (Security Only)

For additional protection, your IP address is temporarily logged when you perform security-sensitive actions such as a login or “forgot password” request. This data is used only for verification and abuse prevention and is automatically cleared after a short period (usually within 48 hours). You can request deletion at any time.

3. Why We Collect This Data

  • To verify your identity and secure your Dredbot account
  • To notify your Discord account of login attempts or suspicious activity
  • To prevent unauthorized access and maintain service reliability
  • To provide essential features such as password recovery

4. Discord Integration

Dredbot relies on Discord's API for authentication. We never request unnecessary permissions or access to your messages or servers. Any information shared through Discord remains governed by the Discord Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies & Sessions

We use secure session cookies solely to maintain your login state. These cookies:

  • Keep you securely signed in
  • Expire automatically when you log out or close your browser
  • Do not track browsing or collect analytics

6. Third-Party Services

Dredbot uses Discord for identity verification. No other third-party analytics, ad trackers, or marketing tools are used. We do not sell, share, or rent user data to any external services.

7. Data Retention

Session data and temporary IP logs are stored only as long as necessary for security purposes. Regular user data (e.g., Discord ID) is retained only while your Dredbot account is active. You can request deletion of all stored information at any time by contacting us on Discord.

8. Data Security

All communications with Dredbot use HTTPS encryption. Sensitive data, including session tokens, are securely stored and hashed where applicable. Access is restricted to authorized system processes only.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights under data protection laws such as the GDPR or CCPA, including:

  • The right to request a copy of your stored data
  • The right to correct or update your information
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to data processing for non-essential purposes

To exercise these rights, please contact us via Discord: pshsayhi4117.

10. Children's Privacy

Dredbot is intended for Discord users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). If we learn that such data has been collected, it will be promptly deleted.

11. Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect service changes or legal requirements. When updates occur, we'll revise the “Effective Date” above. Continued use of Dredbot after updates means you accept the revised terms.

12. Contact

If you have privacy questions or data requests, contact us via Discord: pshsayhi4117 or through our Discord Server.
